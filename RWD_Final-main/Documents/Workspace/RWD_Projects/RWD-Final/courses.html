HBU offers a variety of courses that cater to the interests and needs of our students. Whether you want to learn the basics of baking and cooking, or advance your skills and knowledge in a specific area, we have a course for you. Our courses are taught by experienced and qualified instructors who are passionate about their subjects and eager to share their expertise with you. Our courses are also designed to be practical and hands-on, so you can apply what you learn in the classroom to real-life situations. Below is a list of some of the courses we offer at HBU. For more information on each course, please click on the course name.

Course Name Course Description
Hawaiian Bread 101 An introduction to the history, culture, and techniques of making Hawaiian bread. Learn how to make the classic sweet and fluffy bread, as well as different variations and flavors.
Baking Science A course that covers the basic principles and concepts of baking, such as ingredients, measurements, methods, and reactions. Learn how to apply scientific knowledge to improve your baking skills and results.
Pastry Arts A course that teaches the art and craft of making various types of pastries, such as pies, tarts, cakes, cookies, and more. Learn how to create beautiful and delicious desserts that will impress your customers and guests.
Culinary Fundamentals A course that covers the essential skills and techniques of cooking, such as knife skills, stocks, sauces, soups, eggs, meats, poultry, fish, and more. Learn how to prepare and present a variety of dishes that will satisfy your palate and appetite.
International Cuisine A course that explores the cuisines and cultures of different regions and countries around the world, such as Asia, Europe, Latin America, and more. Learn how to cook and appreciate the diversity and richness of global food.
Hawaiian Cuisine A course that focuses on the cuisine and culture of Hawaii, which is influenced by various ethnic groups and traditions. Learn how to make and enjoy the local specialties, such as poke, lau lau, kalua pig, spam musubi, and more.
Food and Beverage Management A course that covers the management and operation of food and beverage businesses, such as restaurants, bakeries, cafes, bars, and more. Learn how to plan, organize, control, and evaluate the various aspects of running a successful food and beverage business.
Food Safety and Sanitation A course that covers the safety and sanitation practices and regulations in the food industry, such as foodborne illnesses, hazards, prevention, and control. Learn how to ensure the health and well-being of yourself, your employees, and your customers.
Food Photography and Styling A course that teaches the skills and techniques of taking and editing professional-quality photos of food, as well as styling and arranging food for optimal visual appeal. Learn how to showcase your food creations and attract more customers and followers.
Food Writing and Criticism A course that teaches the skills and techniques of writing and reviewing food, such as blogs, articles, recipes, books, and more. Learn how to express your opinions and insights on food in an engaging and informative way.
Bread Sculpting A course that teaches the art and craft of sculpting bread into various shapes and forms, such as animals, flowers, buildings, and more. Learn how to use different types of dough, tools, and techniques to create stunning and edible bread sculptures.
Chocolate Making A course that teaches the science and art of making chocolate, from bean to bar. Learn how to source, roast, grind, temper, and mold chocolate, as well as how to make different types of chocolate products, such as truffles, bonbons, bars, and more.
Cake Decorating A course that teaches the skills and techniques of decorating cakes, such as piping, fondant, gumpaste, modeling, and more. Learn how to design and create beautiful and delicious cakes for various occasions, such as birthdays, weddings, holidays, and more.
Bread and Cheese Pairing A course that teaches the principles and practices of pairing bread and cheese, two of the most popular and versatile foods in the world. Learn how to select, taste, and serve different types of bread and cheese, as well as how to pair them with other foods and beverages.
Vegan Baking A course that teaches the basics and benefits of vegan baking, which is baking without using any animal products, such as eggs, dairy, honey, and more. Learn how to substitute and use various plant-based ingredients, such as flax seeds, almond milk, coconut oil, and more, to make delicious and healthy vegan baked goods.
Gluten Free Baking A course that teaches the basics and benefits of gluten free baking, which is baking without using any wheat or other gluten-containing grains, such as barley, rye, and more. Learn how to substitute and use various gluten free flours, such as rice, almond, oat, and more, to make delicious and healthy gluten free baked goods.
Sourdough Baking A course that teaches the art and science of sourdough baking, which is baking with natural yeast that is cultivated from flour and water. Learn how to make and maintain your own sourdough starter, as well as how to use it to make various types of sourdough bread and other baked goods.
Bread Machine Baking A course that teaches the convenience and advantages of bread machine baking, which is baking with an electric appliance that mixes, kneads, proofs, and bakes bread automatically. Learn how to use and program your bread machine, as well as how to make various types of bread and other baked goods with it.
Pizza Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making pizza, one of the most popular and beloved foods in the world. Learn how to make different types of pizza dough, sauce, cheese, and toppings, as well as how to bake and slice pizza.
Sandwich Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making sandwiches, one of the most versatile and convenient foods in the world. Learn how to make different types of bread, spreads, fillings, and garnishes, as well as how to assemble and cut sandwiches.
Salad Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making salads, one of the most healthy and refreshing foods in the world. Learn how to make different types of salad greens, vegetables, fruits, nuts, seeds, cheese, and dressing, as well as how to toss and serve salads.
Soup Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making soups, one of the most comforting and nourishing foods in the world. Learn how to make different types of soup bases, stocks, broths, creams, and purees, as well as how to add and cook various ingredients, such as vegetables, meats, grains, beans, and more.
Pasta Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making pasta, one of the most popular and satisfying foods in the world. Learn how to make different types of pasta dough, shapes, and fillings, as well as how to cook and sauce pasta.
Rice Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making rice, one of the most staple and versatile foods in the world. Learn how to make different types of rice, such as white, brown, basmati, jasmine, and more, as well as how to cook and flavor rice.
Bread Pudding Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making bread pudding, one of the most delicious and economical desserts in the world. Learn how to make different types of bread pudding, such as classic, chocolate, caramel, and more, as well as how to bake and serve bread pudding.
French Toast Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making french toast, one of the most popular and easy breakfasts in the world. Learn how to make different types of french toast, such as plain, cinnamon, banana, and more, as well as how to cook and serve french toast.
Pancake Making A course that teaches the skills and techniques of making pancakes, one of the most fluffy and fun breakfasts in the world. Learn how to make different types of pancakes, such as buttermilk, blueberry, chocolate chip, and more, as well as how to cook and serve pancakes.
