HBU is proud to have a team of talented and dedicated staff who work hard to provide our students with the best education and experience possible. Our staff come from diverse backgrounds and have various qualifications and achievements in the fields of baking, culinary arts, hospitality, and tourism. They are also passionate about teaching and sharing their knowledge and skills with our students. Below is a list of some of the staff members at HBU.
